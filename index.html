THE STORY BEHIND PICKIE

Why I built Pickie.

My first pickleball game was August 2024 in Ho Chi Minh City. A friend dragged me along "just to try it." By the last rally, I was hooked.

One game became every game. Casual social sessions turned into competitive ones, and competitive ones turned into tournaments across cities in Indonesia and Vietnam. Along the way I partnered with more players than I can count, and the same struggle followed me to every court: finding a partner you truly click with is hard. Not just a similar level. Real chemistry, the kind that makes a game flow.

So I turned everything those matches taught me into Pickie's chemistry algorithm, built for players who want more partners who fit and for anyone just picking up a paddle. Whether you play for the social side, for tournaments, or just for fun, Pickie helps you connect with the people who fit your game.

"Pickleball is fun. The right chemistry makes it even more fun."

Kevin's hope is bigger than one app: grow pickleball from Indonesia and Vietnam across Southeast Asia and far beyond it — everywhere in the world a net goes up.
